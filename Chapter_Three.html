3.1.6 Exercise 3.6

rand-update is currently undefined, so we’ll define it as inc.

> (define rand-update inc)

Then:

> (define new-rand
    (let ([last 0])
      (lambda (op)
        (cond
          [(eq? op 'generate)
           (set! last (rand-update last))
           last]
          [(eq? op 'reset)
           (lambda (new-last) (set! last new-last))]
          [else
           (error "Unknown operation -- NEW-RAND"
                  op)]))))

Examples:
> (new-rand 'generate)

1

> (new-rand 'generate)

2

> ((new-rand 'reset) 100)
> (new-rand 'generate)

101
